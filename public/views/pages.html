The Mapping Unit

It'll blow your mind

The mapping unit will be used to record current status of a set of E.M. bands. This historical data will be used to create a model to help predict future status of the bands given enviornment parameters. This will allow us to predict which bands should be used now. Presented to the right is the mapping unit frame and components.

The Delivery Unit

Oh yeah, it's that good

A hexcopter uav will be used to move the mapping unit from location to location. This will allow our solution to remain mobile and offer a wider range of usable features. The uav is powered using 6 high power motors for a total lift thrust of over 12 lbs. This can lift a heavy payload with enough batteries for long term sampling.

The Statistical Unit

Checkmate

A multinomial logistic regression model will be used as the future prediction model. The model will use the current second, minute, hour, day and weather conditions as the predictor variables. The best band to use will be reported as the predicted variable. Other classification machine learning methods such as decision trees and nerual networks will be tested. (The scikit learn library will be used.)
